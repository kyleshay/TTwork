Work mode for TT! Hide all the rave stuff, so that it doesn't you're playing a game.

How to use:
  1. Drag this link: TTw to your bookmark bar.
  2. Click the bookmarket with TT open.
Surpise! A new item has been added to your turntable menu.

To enable/disable:
  1. Click the 'WorkMode' button in the turntable menu
  2. Click the TTw bookmark again
