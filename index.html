Work mode for TT! Hide all the rave stuff, so that it doesn't look like you're playing a game.

How to use:
  1. Drag this link: TTw to your bookmark bar.
  2. Click the TTw bookmarklet with turntable open from your bookmarks bar.
Surpise! A new item has been added to your turntable menu.

To enable/disable:
  1. Click the WorkMode button in the turntable menu.
  2. Click the TTw bookmarklet again.
